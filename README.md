## 📖 About decentralized-forex-broker

decentralized-forex-broker is a software that lets you trade popular currency pairs like EUR/USD, GBP/USD, and USD/JPY. It works on a blockchain platform, meaning no central company controls your trades. Instead, the system uses smart contracts and real-time price feeds to give you accurate pricing and secure trading.

This application uses vault-based liquidity pools. That means your money and others' money are pooled safely and earn you revenue based on trading activity. The prices are set using oracles, which are trusted data sources from the outside world, so you get fair and up-to-date rates. The protocol also has multiple ways to generate earnings through trading fees and liquidity rewards.

The app offers an easy way to trade synthetic forex contracts. Synthetic means you do not own the actual currencies but trade contracts that track their prices. This lets you benefit from price movements without handling real cash or banks.
